I Square Taxi Recommends - 5 Things that you cannot miss in Hosur

Book a car rental in Hosur for outstation and travel your heart out. Some of the places near Hosur you must visit are-

  1. Learn about Jain history in Tamil Nadu at the Shree Parshwa Susheel Dham Swetamber Jain Temple, a prominent local temple and spiritual complex, famous for its fine marble and stone carvings.
  2. For a dose of uninterrupted nature, spend a day at the Kelavarapelli Reservoir, an artificial reservoir set amidst lush greenery, nature trails, and a prominent dam. You can blaze the trails or have a picnic by the reservoir. The best time to visit is during the winter months or early spring.

  4. Trek to the Brahma hills, the highest point of Hosur, famous for day treks and panoramic views.
  5. Wish to go to Tirupati but lacking the time? Then Dakshina Tirupati in Hosur might partly fulfill your desire. The sacred site with natural surroundings serves as a smaller version of the Tirumala Tirupati Temple and draws a lot of pilgrims.
