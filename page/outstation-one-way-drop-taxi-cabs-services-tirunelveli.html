Due to the nature of outstation travel, most trips are pre-scheduled and pre-booked enabling us to arrange for the right vehicle for your journey. What is included as part of the charges for an outstation trip?

As part of an outstation journey, your charges include a certain number of pre-allocated km and the applicable taxes for the journey.

For one-way trips from Tirunelveli- we may include the applicable tolls as your route of travel is pre-defined.

In the case of round trips or multi-day journeys starting in Tirunelveli, the customer is expected to pay for all applicable tolls along the way. For a roundtrip or multi-day journeys for an outstation cab from Tirunelveli, you may use the vehicle up to the allocated time and number of km without any extra km driving charges.

Typically a one-way trip is a point to point transfer between two cities. We need you to provide us the exact pickup and drop addresses. Your price quotation is based on the estimated km of travel between the two locations assuming there will be no intermediate stops or waypoints or sightseeing. It's simply an intercity transfer from A to B in a cab that is for your use. You do not share the cab with anyone else.
